FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers.

Everything you need to know before getting started — no hidden surprises.

Do I need to be on WordPress to use WebCareLeader?

Most of our clients are on WordPress, which is our core specialty. We also support Webflow, Squarespace, and custom HTML/CSS sites on our Business plan. Not sure if we support your platform? Request a free checkup and we'll let you know within 24 hours.

What exactly is a "free website checkup"?

It's a no-obligation audit of your website covering: security vulnerabilities, plugin/theme update status, page speed score, uptime history, SSL certificate, and broken links. We send you a plain-English report with findings and recommendations — whether or not you become a client.

How long does it take to get started?

Once you sign up, we complete onboarding within 48 hours. We'll ask for limited admin access to your website (we explain exactly what we need and why), run an initial audit, and apply any urgent fixes. From that point, your site is actively maintained on a rolling basis.

Is my website safe if I give you admin access?

Absolutely. We follow strict security protocols — your credentials are stored in an enterprise-grade password vault, never in email or spreadsheets. Our team operates on a need-to-know basis, and you can revoke access at any time. We carry professional liability insurance for added peace of mind.

Can I cancel my plan at any time?

Yes. All plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. You can cancel before your next billing date and you won't be charged again. We'll even provide a full handover document so your site stays in great shape after you leave.

What happens if my site gets hacked while I'm on a plan?

We handle it at no extra charge. On all plans, if your site is compromised while under our care, we will clean the malware, restore from backup, patch the vulnerability, and harden the security — covered within your monthly plan. We've never had a client on an active plan experience an unresolved breach.

I already have a web developer. Do I still need this?

Most web developers build sites — they're not set up for ongoing, day-to-day maintenance. WebCareLeader complements your developer perfectly: we handle the routine work (updates, backups, monitoring, reports) so your developer can focus on new features and growth projects. Many of our clients were referred to us directly by their developers.

Do you offer custom or enterprise plans?

Yes. If you run multiple websites, a high-traffic eCommerce store, or need custom SLA terms, get in touch and we'll build a plan that fits. Start with a free checkup and we'll discuss your specific requirements.
